about=Wincan Vx to TEKSI Importer allows importing pipe inspection TV data in Wincan Vx format into TEKSI Wastewater module. Wincan is a software for the inspection and management of the waste water infrastructure (http://www.wincan.com/). The data respects the EN 13508 norm. You can browse inspection data and review each observation and choose what will be imported. Data will be imported into a TWW project which has a data model respecting the Swiss-norm VSA-DSS.
